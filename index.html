We are among the highest users of electronics in the world, and unwanted electronics are one of the fastest growing types of waste.

"E-Waste: A Global Challenge, A Local Responsibility"

E-waste, short for electronic waste, encompasses discarded electrical and electronic devices. These include outdated computers, smartphones, appliances, and more. As technology advances at an astonishing pace, the accumulation of e-waste becomes a pressing concern.
