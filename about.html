About us


Welcome to E-Waste Facility Locator !



Who We Are

At E-Waste Facility Locator, we are passionate about creating a sustainable future by addressing the growing issue of electronic waste (e-waste). We believe that responsible e-waste management is not just a necessity but a responsibility that each one of us shares.


Our Mission

Promote E-Waste Awareness: We aim to educate individuals, businesses, and communities about the environmental and social impact of e-waste and the importance of recycling and proper disposal.
Facilitate Responsible Recycling: We provide resources and information to help you responsibly recycle your old electronics, ensuring they are diverted from landfills and processed in an environmentally friendly manner.
Support Environmental Sustainability: We advocate for sustainable practices in e-waste recycling and work with certified partners to ensure that e-waste is managed in an environmentally responsible way.


What We Do

E-Waste Collection Events: E-Waste Facility Locator regularly organizes e-waste collection events in collaboration with local communities, making it easy for individuals and businesses to dispose of their electronic waste safely.
Education and Awareness: We offer comprehensive resources, articles, and guides to help you understand the e-waste problem, how it affects the planet, and what you can do to be part of the solution.
Certified Recycling Partners: We partner with certified e-waste recycling facilities that adhere to strict environmental standards, ensuring that your old electronics are recycled responsibly.


Why E-Waste Matters

Electronic waste is one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally, and improper disposal can lead to severe environmental and health hazards. By recycling your old electronics and supporting responsible e-waste management, you play a vital role in reducing pollution, conserving resources, and protecting our planet for future generations.


Join Us in Making a Difference

We invite you to join us in our mission to combat e-waste and promote sustainability. Together, we can make a significant impact on the environment and create a better world for all.
